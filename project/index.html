EvOCRO Project

Understanding the evolutionary origins of cognition in crocodiles through fieldwork, behavioral assays, and comparative analyses.

Publications Team

Objectives

  • Map key cognitive domains across life stages.
  • Link behavior to ecology and life history.
  • Share open datasets and reproducible protocols.

Methods

Field experiments, ethograms, automated tracking, preregistrations, and transparent analysis pipelines.

Outputs

Publications, datasets, outreach (blog/vlog), workshops, and training activities.

Work Packages

The EvOCRO project is structured in interconnected work packages (WPs) focusing on cognition, development, comparative evolution, and public engagement. Each WP combines fieldwork, experimental testing, and data integration.
